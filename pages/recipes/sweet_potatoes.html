Roasted Sweet Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme

Instructions

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). In a large bowl, toss the sweet potatoes with olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and thyme until evenly coated. Spread the sweet potatoes on a baking sheet in a single layer. Roast for 25-30 minutes, stirring halfway through, until the sweet potatoes are tender and golden brown. Serve warm and enjoy!
