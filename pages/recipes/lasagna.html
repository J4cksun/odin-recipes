Classic Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 ounces marinara sauce
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil or parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, drain, and set aside. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat, add ground beef, onion, and garlic, and cook until beef is browned. Stir in marinara sauce, then simmer for 5 minutes. In a bowl, mix ricotta, 1 cup of mozzarella, Parmesan, egg, salt, and pepper. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of sauce, layer noodles, ricotta mixture, sauce, and repeat until the dish is full, ending with sauce and remaining mozzarella on top. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove foil and bake another 15 minutes until bubbly. Let sit for 10 minutes, garnish with basil or parsley, and serve.
